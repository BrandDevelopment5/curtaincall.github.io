Loeki Reviewed Les Miserables

"Les Misérables" in the Netherlands transports audiences into Victor Hugo's poignant world with a mesmerizing adaptation that does justice to the timeless tale. From the moment the curtains rise, viewers are enraptured by the raw emotion, powerhouse performances, and breathtaking set design. The Dutch rendition skillfully captures the essence of Hugo's masterpiece while infusing it with a distinct local flair, creating a production that is both faithful to the original and refreshingly unique. With its stirring music, compelling narrative, and unforgettable characters, this adaptation of "Les Misérables" is a triumph of storytelling and a must-see for theater enthusiasts of all ages. It's a production that lingers in the heart and mind long after the final curtain call, leaving audiences spellbound and deeply moved.

Kira Reviewed Pretty Woman

This was the absolutely greatest play I’ve seen in my lifetime. I’ve seen thousands of plays and this is now my new all-time favourite. I believe Shanna Slaap, who played Vivian was beyond amazing. I cannot believe such talent and such fantastic singing could come from one woman who is the perfect one to be starred as the lead. The co star, Jan Kooijman did an outstanding job as well. If you have to see one play in the Netherlands this year, I strongly recommend Pretty Woman the musical.
